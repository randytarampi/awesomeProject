Scheduler Instructions

This app is pretty buggy, so use at your own risk.

That said, this was always going to be more of a proof of concept than workable application.

This application's most basic functions are presented right up front and centre on the scheduler home page. We hope it's intuitive enough, but just in case, the user has to do two things:

  1. Select some set of classes, either
    • by specifying a subject and course number (e.g. CMPT 300) or
    • by specifying a subject an instructor and then a course number (e.g. CMPT 320 by Christopher Bastone).
  2. Clicking 'Generate Schedule'
      Generally this will popup some sort of weekly schedule below, under the 'Your Optimal Schedule' heading with some information.

Should the user feel more adventurous he or she can search for classes by subject or instructor. The user may also notice that the URLs are pretty hackable - the URL to find Steven Pearce's details ends with his SFU computing ID, 'stevenp', and to find all offered sections of CMPT 300, one need only to tack '/subject/CMPT/300' to our home page URL. Note that if only one section is offered, the user will be redirected directly to the course detail page as in the case of CMPT 320.
